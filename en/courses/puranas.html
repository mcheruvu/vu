Academic Courses in Purānna

AsTadasha (18) Purānnas

There are 18 (AsTadasha) Purānnas. Purānna means that which is very ancient. We will be studying these Purānnas and ascertain the slokas that fall within the purview of Vēdas.

  • 1. Brahma Purānna (P-01)

  • 2. Padma Purānna (P-02)

  • 3. Vishnu Purānna (P-03)

  • 4. Shiva Purānna (P-04)

  • 5. Bhagavata Purānna (P-05)

  • 6. Nārada Purānna (P-06)

  • 7. Markandēya Purānna (P-07)

  • 8. Agni Purānna (P-08)

  • 9. Bhavishya Purānna (P-09)

  • 10. Brahma Vaivartha Purānna (P-10)

  • 11. Linga Purānna (P-11)

  • 12. Varāha Purānna (P-12)

  • 13. Skānda Purānna (P-13)

  • 14. Vāmana Purānna (P-14)

  • 15. Kūrma Purānna (P-15)

  • 16. Matsya Purānna (P-16)

  • 17. Garuda Purānna (P-17)

  • 18. Brahmānda Purānna (P-18)
