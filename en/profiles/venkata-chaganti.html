Modern Physics

Dr. Venkata Chaganti’s Ph.D. research:
Dr. Venkata R Chaganti is a doctorate in condensed matter physics from Georgia State University. Quantum dots strongly exhibit size-dependent optical and electrical properties. The ability to join the dots into complex assemblies creates many opportunities for scientific discovery. This motivated his research work on QDIPs, DWELLs, and graphene like QDs. The intention of his research was to study the size dependent achievements of QDIPs, DWELLs, and graphene like QDs with those of competitive technologies, with the emphasis on the material properties, device structure, and their impact on the device performance.

Dr. Venkata Chaganti taught undergraduate physics courses (both lab and classroom) in USA college/universities for about 11 years. He worked as an IT consultant and Biomedical Engineer. His work as Biomedical Engineer is to Sales and Service of Biomedical Equipment like ECG, EEG, Intra-Aortic Balloon Pumps, Stress Test equipment, Monitors etc. As an IT consultant he worked in C, C++, Java, FORTRAN, Android App development.
