तस्मा॑द्य॒ज्ञात्स॑र्व॒हुत॒ ऋचः॒ सामा॑नि जज्ञिरे ।
छन्दां॑सि जज्ञिरे॒ तस्मा॒द्यजु॒स्तस्मा॑दजायत ॥९॥

सत् = The one who has no death, चित् = Who is ever knowledgeable, who does not have even an iota of ignorance, आनद = Who is always in bliss and never gets any pain, is called सर्वहुत. That means He is the one with all capabilities. From such a परब्रह्मन् the Vedas (ऋचः) Rigvēda, (सामानि) Samavēda (जज्ञिरे) born. (तस्मात्) from that Brahma, (छंदांसि) Atharvavēda (जज्ञिरे) born. (तस्मात्) from that Brahma (यजुः) Yajurvēda (अजायत) were born.
Rigvēda (10.90.9)
