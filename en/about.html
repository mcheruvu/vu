About Us

Our university is a Vedic-centered organization delivering excellence in knowledge.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower students to value Vedic knowledge and skills that will support them as life-long learners.

The impact will be on trained doctors, engineers, scientists, politicians, economists, farmers, teachers, philosophers, etc.

Our Mission

What do we do?
  • Community: We build an enthusiastic creative community of vedic learners.
  • Quality: We provide relevant high-quality knowledge transfer of Vedas.
  • Excellence: We are dedicated to a continuing vedic tradition of excellence in an ever changing world.
  • Vedic-Centered: Our university is a vedic-centered organization delivering excellence in knowledge.
How do we do?
  • Research Focused: The mission of the university is to be at the forefront of the advancement of knowledge in the 21st Century through ground-breaking Vedic research agendas that will give our world the tools that they need to live healthy, productive, prosperous, joyful, and meaningful lives.
  • Peer Learning: Learners work in groups that help communication and teamwork. Hearing the perspectives of team members helps learners to see things from multiple perspectives.
  • Project Based Learning: Learners are given short/long term Vedic knowledge-based projects to get a chance to investigate complex problems and identify real-world solutions.
  • Discovery: Learners discover solution to problems themselves instead of being told the answers.

Graduates will be motivated life-long learners and productive global Vedic citizens.

Founder: Dr. Venkata Chaganti

Dr. Venkata R Chaganti, founder, has PhD in Physics with Quantum Dots Specialization from Georgia State University. His vast experience includes teaching and serving AI/ML industries in various capacities. Currently Dr. Chaganti is working as Chief Scientific Officer for a few startup companies which focusses on finding food allergies, medical diagnostics, and environmental aspects by using Vedic, AI, and Nano Sciences. He also worked on medical industry in relation to various medical equipment.

Dr. Venkata Chaganti, the president of Vedas World Inc, a non-profit organization established in Atlanta, USA, focusing on propagating Vedic concepts and doing scientific evidence-based research on Vedic principles. Dr. Chaganti did research in Vedas for more than 38 years and more than 30 years of experience in performing Yajna rituals. Through Vedas World, Dr. Chaganti, has been continuously educating people on Vedic aspects through their website: www.vedas.us, www.vedas.world and YouTube Channel (Venkata Chaganti, Vedas World) with more than 700 videos so far since 2015.

Dr. Venkata Chaganti is also serving as president for Vedas WIKI, a non-profit organization focussing on providing digital tranformation to vedic mantras.
