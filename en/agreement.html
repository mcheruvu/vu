Student Agreement / Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

This Student Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between the University of Applied Vedic Sciences ("University") and the student ("Student") who enrolls in the Certificate Course/Ph.D. program.

Gmail Account Requirement

Every student is required to have a Gmail account for communication purposes with the University.

Non-Disclosure of Study Materials

Study materials, including videos, documents, audios, and any other forms of content shared by the University, must not be shared with anyone else. Sharing of materials may result in disciplinary action.

Email Password Security

Students must not share their email passwords with anyone. Your email account is your responsibility, and you are solely responsible for its security.

Course Intent

The Certificate Course/Ph.D. is intended for learning courses that are specified in the Student Handbook provided by the University.

Qualification for Additional Courses

Students who successfully complete the mandatory courses (Siksha and Ashthadhyayi) will be offered the opportunity to enroll in other courses.

Course Availability

Courses are offered based on the availability of faculty and study materials. Therefore, not all courses may be available immediately.

Study Material Access

Course materials are made available based on tuition fee payments. If the University offers installment payment options, study materials will be released incrementally after each installment is paid.

Examination and Assessment

After completing periodic assignments and exams, there will be a final exam, which may consist of both written and oral components.

Online Assignments and Exams

Assignments and exams will be conducted online. The oral interview may take place through online conferencing or at a location where the student, instructor, and panel can meet.

Participation in Online Discussions

If the University provides an online discussion board, students are required to actively participate, share, and engage with other students as part of the learning process.

Time Limits

Sufficient time will be allocated for each course, and there will be specific time limits for course completion, periodic assignments/exams, and the final exam and oral interview.

NDA for Ph.D. Program

When entering the Ph.D. program after successfully completing the Certificate course, the student will be required to enter into a separate Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) and Intellectual Property Rights Agreement with the University of Applied Vedic Sciences.

By enrolling in the University's programs, the Student acknowledges and agrees to abide by the terms and conditions outlined in this Agreement. Violation of these terms may result in academic or legal consequences.
